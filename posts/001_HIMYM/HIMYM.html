The Verdict

Each version loses impact. By simply changing the meaning of words, the LLM was not able to capture the true sentiment and feel of the script. This makes me believe even more that chatbots are purely pattern matchers and cannot generate creative information that is not just a slight alteration from the base case.

Final Takeaways

Can LLMs match tone and subtext?

LLMs can match tone and subtext, but they do not understand the complexities of language and pop culture over time. The originality of a time period is not easily matched. Instead of adapting an iconic plot to a time period, the LLM adapted specific phrases and words to a time period.

If I had prompted the LLM and emphasized creativity, then perhaps it would have wandered more with the script and created something that the different audiences would actually like.
